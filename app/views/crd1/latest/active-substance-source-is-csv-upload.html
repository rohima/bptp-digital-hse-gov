How do you want to provide your active substance source information?

You can use the CRD active substance source template (opens in a new tab) to enter this information and upload it.

If you do not want to upload a file, you can enter the information manually.

If you choose to upload this information:

  • all sources must have previously been assessed for technical equivalence
  • you must enter the information into the CRD active substance source template before you upload it
