Product risk and microbials

Is the product, including all active substances, considered low-risk?

{#

A plant protection product is generallty considered low risk if it does not have any harmful effects on:

  • human health
  • animal health
  • groundwater
  • the environment
#}

A low-risk active substance in a plant protection product must comply with specific regulatory criteria, and the products containing that substance expected to be only a low risk to human and animal health and the environment.

Find out more about low-risk active substances on the HSE website (opens in a new tab).
