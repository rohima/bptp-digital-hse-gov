{# {{ serviceName }} #} Manage pesticide product authorisations

If you are planning to market, import or carry out research and development work with a plant protection product (pesticides) in Great Britain or Northern Ireland, you can use this service to:

  • apply for authorisation for a new product
  • apply to renew or make a change to an existing product
  • make a change to an existing authorisation
  • apply for an extension of authorisation for minor use
  • apply for, or make a change to, an inclusion on the ‘Official List’ of adjuvants
  • apply for standalone technical equivalence
  • apply for an emergency authorisation
  • apply to change Maximum Residue Levels (MRLs)
