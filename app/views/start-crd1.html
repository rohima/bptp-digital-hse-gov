{# {{ serviceName }} #} Manage pesticide product authorisations

If you are planning to market a plant protection product (pesticides) in Great Britain and/or Northern Ireland, you can use this service to apply for authorisation for a new product.

The Health and Safety Executive’s (HSE) Chemicals Regulation Division (CRD) will evaluate applications submitted on this service.

{#

If you are planning to market a plant protection product (pesticides) in Great Britain and/or Northern Ireland, you can use this service to:

  • apply for authorisation for a new product
  • apply to renew an existing product
  • make a change to an existing product
  • apply for an extension of authorisation for minor use (EAMU)
  • apply for standalone technical equivalence of an active substance source (Great Britain only)
  • apply for an emergency authorisation
  • apply to change Maximum Residue Levels (MRLs)
#} {{ govukButton({ text: "Start now", href: "application-reference", isStartButton: true }) }}

Before you start

You will be asked to upload supporting documentation within this application.

You will need to upload your formulation composition, active substance source details and proposed use information (GAP table) as .csv files, using [CRD templates].

{#

For most applications you will need to upload full details of the technical specification and formulation recipe. Find out more about technical specifications and formulation recipes on the HSE website (link opens in a new tab) .
