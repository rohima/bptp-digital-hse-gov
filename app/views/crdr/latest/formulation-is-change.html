Is there a change to the currently authorised product formulation, other than a non-significant formulation change?

The only changes permitted at renewal are the following non-significant formulation changes:

  • alternative sources of co-formulant
  • cation exchange in an anionic surfactant or dispersant
  • addition of a previously agreed marker substance
Help with non-significant formulation changes
You can find out more about the permitted non-significant formulation changes on the HSE website (opens in a new tab)
